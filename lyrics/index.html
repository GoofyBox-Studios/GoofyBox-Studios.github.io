Pronciation Key

  • In "One in a day", the "in" and the "a" are silent.
  • In "crushing", the "sh" is pronounced as "s" and the "u" is pronouced oo and the "ing" is pronounce in' (Two links in "in'")
  • In "my math", both "m"s are silent, the "y" is pronounced as "uh" (without "h"), the "a" is an "l", the "th" is pronounced "ong" (Silent "g")
  • The "I" is silent, the first "p" is pronounced as a "j" (Not "Jay", just the first sound), the "ep" is pronounced "oo"
  • Typo: "calculator" is actually "song"
  • The "Sc" is pronounced "B", while the "m" is pronounced as "th"
